This form allows you to change the email associated your account. Here's an explanation of the fields and buttons:
  This is the email that is currently associated with your account. The value is filled in by the program.
  Enter the password associated with this account.
  Enter the new email to be associated with this account.
>   Press this button to submit your change request. If successful, you will receive a brief notification and the user status in the About Menu will reflect the new email address.
