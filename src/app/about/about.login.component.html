The Account Sign In form allows you to sign in to your account. Here's an explanation of the fields and buttons:
  Enter the email address to be associated with this account. The value must be in the correct format for an email address.
  Enter the password to be associated with this account. This value must be between 6 and 16 characters.
  Check this box if you wish to use the email and password to create a new account. When checked, this will reveal the password confirmation field.
  Reenter the value given in the Password field. These values must match exactly.
  Check this box to have your Email and Password automatically filled in for you when you log in.
  Press this button to submit your information for verification. If successful, you will receive a brief notification and the user icon at the top of the page will become solid. Also, the user status on the About Menu will show your email address.
  If the password entered is incorrect, this button will appear. Pressing this button will give you the option to receive an email containing a temporary password that can be used to sign in.
  To retry your sign in, enter your password again and press the Sign In Button.
